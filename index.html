About Me

Hi and welcome! I'm an environmetal and social conscious strategist from Barcelona. I analyse, develop and implement innovative strategies to improve customers' experience with digital platforms with the ultimate goal of customer happiness, engagement and growth.

I graduated in International Business Economics at Universitat Pompeu Fabra, Barcelona and then I moved to Australia to further develop myself as a young professional overseas. I have experience in the tech industry after working in several startups where I learnt to work in dynamic and cross-functional teams and agile environments. I'm particularly interested in leveraging disruptive technologies to create people-driven economies, and true believed of the power of individual consumes' to infruence change through purchasing decisions.

I'm currently studying a MSc in Innovationa and Global Sustainable Development at Lund University and are furthering my Data Science skills. I aspire to leverage data's storytelling potential to create change in the world.
